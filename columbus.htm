About Columbus



Columbus is located in central Ohio and is the most populous city in Ohio and the 14th most in the USA

Columbus is home of the Ohio state unversity and is known for its presence in the college sport's world

household income for the Columbus Ohio metro area was $71,839 in 2021, the latest figures available.

Columbus has 2 proffessional sports teams: Bluejackets - NHL and Columbus Crews - MLS
