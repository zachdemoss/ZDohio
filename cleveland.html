About Cleveland



Cleveland is located in the Northeast of Ohio and is the 2nd most populous of Ohio

Cleveland is located to one of the great lakes (lake Erie)

The median income for one person in Cleveland is $35,470. This is one of the 50 lowest median incomes among Ohio's 293 cities

Cleveland is home of 3 proffessional sports teams: Cavaliers - NBA, Browns - NFL, and the Guardians - MLB

Cleveland is considered to be a urban city
