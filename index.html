About Ohio





Ohio is a state in the midwest region of the United States and shares borders with Indiana, Michigan, Kentucky, and West Virginia. Ohio is also known as the "Buckeye state".

Ohio is a mostly rural state with 3 main cities (Columbus, Cleveland, and Cincinnati) with the rest of the state being pretty much farmlands

Population of Ohio is 11,780,017 and the average income is $54,021 as of 2021
